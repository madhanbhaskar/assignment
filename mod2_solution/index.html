CARNIVORES

Carnivorous are the animals that eat other animals.they are usualy found in wild.The common wild animals are tiger,lion,snak,leopard etc.It is advised not to rear these animals at home as they are very dangerous!

HERBIVORES

Herbivores are animals that eat grass plants and not other animals.They are found both in wild and reared at homes as well.The most common hebivores are cow,goat,hores etc.They are usualy reared at home and not dangerous at all!

OMNIVORES

Omnivores belong to the category where in they eat both plants and animals as well.humans we are the most common examples under this category.the other examples include bear,crow etc! they are dependent both on plants and other animals to survive!
